Summer Activities

Between May and October you can take trips onto the St. Lawrence River and go whale watching. Or if going onto the seaway doesn't tickle your fancy, there are places on the shore line that you can watch for the whales.

During the warmer months of the year taking either guided tours or wandering the streets of old Québec is a wonderful activity. Much of the Québec architecture is of the old European style so walking through Québec will feel like you're walking through an old part of Europe.

If you enjoy canoeing or kayaking Québec provides you with a plethora of options. From voyaging in the sea or floating leisurely down streams, you can have guided tours or take your own path.
