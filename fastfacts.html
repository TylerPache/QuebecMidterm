Fast Facts

Québec is the largest and first province of Canada. Québec City is the capital of Québec.

Montreal is located in the Southern region of Québec and is home to Cirque du Soleil.

Over 95% of the Québec population speaks French as their native language while 40% are fluent in both French and English.

Old Québec City was part of the first fort built by the French in Canada and the original walls of the fort are still standing to this day.

J.A. Moisan Épicier is the oldest grocery store in North America being established in 1871
