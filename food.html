Food

Poutine is a dish that was created and cultivated in Quebec. It is made with french fries topped with gravy, cheese curds and occasionally some type of meat.

Tourtière is another dish created and cultivated in Quebec. It is a pie generally made with pork, veal or beef and potatoes. Wild game is sometimes added to enhance the flavor.

Pâté chinois is a Canadian dish that is very similar to a shepherd's pie.

Pouding chômeur is a Quebec dessert which means poor man's pudding in English. The dessert is similar to a pineapple upside down cake, only without any fruit.

Bûche de Noël is a French dessert with traditions that has been carried over into Quebec. It is probably better known as a Yule log. It is made from a chocolate sponge cake that is rolled and frosted to resemble an actual Yule log.
