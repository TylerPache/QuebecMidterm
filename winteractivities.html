Winter Activities

Village des Sports Snow Park is the largest winter park in North America. It has 35 different sledding tracks, a kilometer long skating track through the parks landscape, a hotel made of ice that you can rent a room in as well as an indoor water park. The claim to fame of the park though is Everest, a 33.5 meter high inner tube slide.

Érablière du Lac-Beauport is one of many sugar shacks providing visitors with a multitude of maple based products as well as live entertainment and a hearty Québécois meal.
