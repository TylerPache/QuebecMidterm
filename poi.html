Points of Interest

Montmorency Falls is located 15 minutes from Old Québec. the 83 meter tall waterfall is the focal point of the landscape whether visiting the park or just driving by, the waterfall is a majestic site to behold.

The Basilica of Saint Anne-de-Beaupré is known as a place of miracles. There is a rich history of people being inexplicably healed after visiting the basilica. The basilica is also home to the shrine of Saint Anne.

Atelier Paré is a wood sculpture museum. The museum provides visitors with a story telling based from a wood carving as well as hand made wood sculptures for sale. There is also the chance to see the sculptures being made.
